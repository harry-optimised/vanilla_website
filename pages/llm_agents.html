LLM Agents Series

This series documents my journey in learning to build embodied LLM agents in Minecraft. This is not a how-to article. I don't know how best to build these agents. But I hope to figure it out by building lots of them and seeing what works. If you follow along you'll see me make many mistakes and go down plenty of rabbit holes, but hopefully we'll see a picture begin to emerge. My goal is to work towards a foundational understanding of the state of the art, notably the Voyager paper.

Why Minecraft? Because it's what the Voyager paper uses, and there are already good tools for building bots for Minecraft, I do not intend to reinvent the wheel. There's also something deeply satisfying about watching your agents explore a proper 3D world.
