Thoughts on building ML applications

Hi, I'm Harry.

I'm the tech lead for the Data Platform team at Vaarst. We're building a data platform in AWS to allow our customers to intelligently access, manage, and analyse geo-referenced offshore data.

This isn't a website about that, however.

I work on personal projects in my spare time and read things that interest me, mostly around Machine Learning and System Design. My passion is designing and building systems with AI and ML at their core, and it's what I'm focussing my career around.

This blog is a place to capture thoughts and ideas relating to that goal, somewhere to keep track of progress, to reflect and write things down that I've learned along the way. Although I'm doing it for me, I'm making it public in case anything here is of use to somebody else.
