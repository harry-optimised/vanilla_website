  • Detecting the Facebook outage with PyBGPStream

    On 4th October 2021, a misconfiguration caused major DNS resolvers to fail when querying facebook.com, resulting in worldwide access loss to Facebook and its associated services. This outage, which appeared as a BGP issue, highlights the importance of the little-known yet vital internet protocol BGP. This blog post delves into BGP and DNS, unpacks the Facebook incident, and demonstrates detecting such outages using PyBGPStream.
