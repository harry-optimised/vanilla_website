Anki Card Maker

22nd May 2023

A quick app I knocked up to automate the process of creating Anki Flashcards from online articles. I use Anki a lot for remembering stuff, and if I read a good online article that I want to remember, I'll try and capture at least three new Anki cards from it. Most of the time, the act of thinking about the card and writing it is helpful for the remembering process, but sometimes it's good to get a bit of help so I started experimenting with ChatGPT. LLMs work well for this, it's essentially just summarisation after all, but the copying and pasting of the prompt and article content got tedious fast. Hence... this app!

Please feel free to copy/fork/clone/modify etc for your own purposes. It's in Python and HTML so should be quick and easy to customise. I wrote it as a web app because the UI is just nicer to use. You can run it from a virtual environment or in a docker container.
